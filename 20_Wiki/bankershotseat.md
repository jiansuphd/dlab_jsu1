# Banker's Hot Seat

**Location:** `10_Projects/bankershotseat/`
**Current Build:** v4.6 [Final Sync]
**Technology:** HTML5, Tailwind CSS, SCORM 1.2

## Overview
A high-stakes credit negotiation simulation where students must secure $250,000 in capital for farm expansion. The game tests the ability to mitigate solvency risks using real-time financial telemetry.

## Features
- **SCORM 1.2 Integration:** Automatic gradebook syncing with Canvas.
- **Telemetry Selection:** Students choose 3 of 6 financial indicators (NFI, DTA, etc.) to build their case.
- **Branching Dialogue:** Adaptive interview with Senior Analyst Henderson.
- **Dynamic HUD:** Real-time "Credibility Index" and telemetry stream tracking.
- **Deep Red Theme:** Branded to AREC 342 (#b73327) specifications.

## Course Alignment
- **Primary Module:** Module 6 (Capital and Credit).
- **Capstone Role:** Module 9 (Integrated Analysis) prep.
- **CLO Alignment:** CLO 6 (Analyze capital requirements and credit options).
